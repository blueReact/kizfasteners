CRITICAL APPLICATION FASTENERS – WHEN FAILURE IS NOT AN OPTION

HOLO-KROME introduces its lineup of MADE IN THE USA Flange 12-Point Screws. Proper fastener selection is critical to completing a successful project. Available in high- strength alloy steel, Holo-Krome Flange 12-Point Screws are an alternative head design for hex socket cap screws.The 12-point design allows for greater torque to be applied to the fastener and have the ability to withstand stretching, deforming and shearing under high stress loads. As with all HOLO-KROME products, these parts are manufactured to withstand heavy duty applications where higher tensile strength is required.

PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS

  • Manufactured and certified to ASTM A574
  • 100% Made in the USA Holo-Krome Quality
  • Alloy Steel
  • Equivalent strength to Socket Head Cap Screw
  • 170,000 – 180,000 PSI Tensile
  • DFARS Compliant / 100% USA Melt
  • RoHS Compliant
